Introduction Last updated: 2021-02-01

Superstore is an easy-to-use retail and inventory management software. It is built with the user in mind that is why the Windows metro design was adopted in both the desktop and the mobile derivates of the system. Superstore makes sales recording fun, and inventory tracking and management swift, due to its unique design and speed of response. Superstore has these major features:

  • Sales Panel (features for making sales that is sent to report for successful sales transactions)
  • Reports (shows details of every sales made, and can be filtered to get more details)
  • Inventory (houses the list of items that are stored with names, prices, barcode, quantity and more)
  • Settings (allow for general configuration of the system, users, and more)
  • Logout (logs out the currently logged-in user)

Sales Panels

Peforming a sale

To perform a sales first you should have logged in and you are in the main menu.

Steps:

  • Click on sales Panel:


  • Type the name of the item to be sold or use the barcode scanner to scan the barcode on the item. (N.B. You must have registered it when entering the item in the inventory).


  • Using the up and down key select the particular item from the list or double-click on the item that is displayed, the enter the quantity to be sold
    •

  • Press the enter key to accept that item.
  • Enter the quantity of the item to be sold and press the enter key.
    • This puts the item into the list. Repeat steps (2) – (5) to add more items to the list.

  • Click on the 'print receipt' button when you are done with the items, then enter the amount given by the customer and press enter to print receipt.
  • Select the payment type made by the customer “cash” or “card” and click the 'Ok' button to finish the transaction.
  • If you have a printer configured it will send it to the printer. There is also the option to disable printing in the situation were you do not desire to print the receipt.
  • N.B. Whether a receipt is printed or not, the sales is already registered in the reports and the item quantity automatically deducted.


    • How to Perform Parallel Sales

    We talked about how to perform a sale, It means that you perform one sale at a time. Parallel sale is when you perform two sales for two customers. One is kept pending while the other runs and vice versa. Each sales hold unique transaction for each customer. Superstore can run two transactions at a time.

    • Go back to sales and do step 2-5 to post items on the list
    • On the right hand side, there are list of buttons, click on the “PAUSE” buttons.
    • The list appears empty and the button starts blinking. This indicates a pending transaction and ready to take new transaction.
    • Repeat step one to add new items to the list.
      • N.B. To review previous transactions that was paused, click the blinking button to view the other transaction.
    • Click print receipt to print the current viewable transaction

    How to Delete an Item from the Sales Panel

    Steps
    • Click on the particular item on the posted list
    • Click on the delete button on the RHS (Right hand side) to delete the item.
    • You will be asked to confirm deletion click “YES” to delete items.
      • N.B. Deletion of an item also affects the total price automatically.

    Report

    Reporting sales

    This feature gives reports of all sales made in the system. It allows you also to filter the reports by day, week, month and even year. You have the option to pick any day or date range and get details of every item that is sold. The port is divided into two major categories.

    • Sales by Item (shows every single item sold within the period selected)
    • Sales by Receipt (show the receipt of the items sold with that period, with an option to view the details which eventually displays the items)
      • The report also displays total sales in figures also does Profit/Loss calculation

      How to Generate a Report

      Steps

      • When on the main menu click on the reports button
      • Select a day/date range from the options listed (Today, Yesterday, This Month, Last Month, Last Three Months etc)
      • Click on the 'view report'
      • The report will display on the right hand side.
        • N.B. If there are no sales made, the report will not show anything






      To get more details on this receipt, click on the 'details' button on the top right corner

    Inventory

    Inventory is the part of superstore that shows list of items and their prices and quantity. You can create a new item, update prices quantity and edit name of the item. The inventory button is visible on the 'Main Menu' when you log in.

    Inventory logo



    Creating an item

    Steps to Create an Item

    • First enter the Itemcode (either manually or scan the barcode) and enter the name of the items. (N.B.: The itemcode is not comulsory you can enter “Item Name” is only.
    • Click on the 'Save' button to create the item
    • Search for the item on the search bar and double-click on the item to enter the New Stock Quantity, Cost Price and Selling Price.
    • Select the expiry date of the item and batch number (if it exists) and click on the 'Update' button to update the Stock Quantity and Prices inputted previously.
    • Continue steps (1) – (4).


    Editing an item name

    How to Edit an Item Name

    Steps:
    • Select the item to be edited in the inventory list. If you cant find the item on the screen, you can search for the item on the 'Find Item' bar and then select the item
    • Click on the options button and click on the edit item and click on 'edit name'
    • On the box that display you can edit both the name of the item and the barcode of the item.
    • When done editing click the 'Save' button

    Settings

    This is the control panel of the whole system and where system configurations and user configurations are made, and managed. The settings menu is made up of five options:

    User roles

    • User settings (for creating and managing user access to the Superstore)
    • Audit Trail (allows an admin track every user activity)
    • Printer settings (gives the option to assign a particular printer to the Superstore)
    • General settings (options to configure print font type, font size, receipt footer, receipt logo and currenty)

    How to create a user

    How to Create a User

    To gain access to use this Superstore, a username and password need to be created. The user is also assigned a particular privilege that determines the type of operation that can be performed. There are 3 types of users: admin (has all rights and privileges), cashier (only makes sales), stockmanager (only manages stock in the inventory).

    Steps

    • On the main menu click on the settings button and select the 'User Settings' option
    • On the left hand side enter the username, password, privilege, and if the user should be active of the new user.
    • Click on the 'save' button to create the user
    • The user displays on the right hand side with the other list of users
      • N.B. The admin has the privileg to disable, delete or change the password of any user
